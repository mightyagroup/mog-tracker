================================================================================
                      DATABASE & MIGRATIONS (1 FILE)
================================================================================

[12] supabase/migrations/033_rbac.sql
     - Complete RBAC database schema migration
     - Creates user_profiles table:
       * id (uuid PK)
       * user_id (uuid, references auth.users)
       * role (text, check constraint)
       * display_name, email
       * entities_access (text array)
       * is_active (boolean)
       * created_at, updated_at (timestamptz)
     - Indexes on user_id and role
     - Updates RLS policies on 8 tables:
       * service_categories
       * gov_leads
       * commercial_leads
       * subcontractors
       * contacts
       * interactions
       * compliance_items
       * pricing_records
     - Role-based RLS policies:
       * Admin: Full access
       * Manager: CRUD on assigned entities
       * Viewer: Read-only on assigned entities
     - Helper SQL functions:
       * get_user_role(uuid)
       * get_user_entities(uuid)
       * user_can_access_entity(uuid, text)
       * user_can_edit(uuid)
     - 450+ lines of SQL
